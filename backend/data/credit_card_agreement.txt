# SpendWise Premium Credit Card Cardmember Agreement

This document outlines the terms and conditions governing the SpendWise Premium Credit Card account.

## Section 1: Interest Rates and Interest Charges

### Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for Purchases
* **Standard Purchase APR:** 19.99% variable. This APR will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate.
* **Introductory Purchase APR:** 0% promotional APR for the first 12 months from account opening. After that, the Standard Purchase APR applies.

### APR for Balance Transfers & Cash Advances
* **Balance Transfer APR:** 21.99% variable.
* **Cash Advance APR:** 26.99% variable. This APR applies immediately upon withdrawal of cash from an ATM using the card.

### Penalty APR and When it Applies
* **Penalty APR:** 29.99% variable.
* **Trigger:** This APR may be applied to your account if you make a late payment or have a payment returned.
* **Duration:** The Penalty APR will apply until you make six consecutive on-time payments starting after the Penalty APR is first applied.

---

## Section 2: Fees

### Annual Membership Fee
* **Annual Fee:** $95, billed annually on your anniversary date.

### Transaction Fees
* **Balance Transfer Fee:** Either $5 or 3% of the amount of each transfer, whichever is greater.
* **Cash Advance Fee:** Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater.
* **Foreign Transaction Fee:** 1% of each transaction in U.S. dollars.

### Penalty Fees
* **Late Payment Fee:** Up to $40. If you do not pay the Minimum Payment due by the payment due date, you will be charged a late fee of $29. If you make another late payment within the next six billing cycles, the late fee increases to $40.
* **Returned Payment Fee:** Up to $35.

---

## Section 3: Billing Errors and Dispute Rights

### Your Right to Dispute Transactions
If you think there is an error on your statement, or if you spot a transaction you did not authorize, you must write to us or submit a dispute online. 

### Critical Dispute Timelines
* **The 60-Day Rule:** We must receive your dispute notice no later than 60 days after we sent you the FIRST statement on which the error or unauthorized charge appeared. If you fail to notify us within this 60-day window, you waive your right to dispute the charge.
* **Acknowledge Receipt:** We will acknowledge your dispute letter in writing within 30 days of receipt, unless we have corrected the error before then.
* **Investigation Window:** We must investigate and resolve the dispute within two complete billing cycles (but no more than 90 days) after receiving your notice.

### Your Status During an Investigation
* **Payment Obligation:** You do not have to pay the disputed amount or any associated interest or fees while we are investigating it. However, you are still obligated to pay the portion of your bill that is not in dispute.
* **Credit Reporting:** We cannot report you as delinquent or take any collection action against you regarding the disputed amount while it is under investigation.

## Section 4: Reward Program Rules

### Earning Cash Back Rewards
* **Base Rate:** Earn 1.5% cash back on all eligible net purchases.
* **Dining & Entertainment Boost:** Earn 3% cash back on eligible dining, restaurants, food delivery services, and streaming subscriptions.
* **Grocery Store Boost:** Earn 2% cash back at eligible supermarkets (excluding wholesale clubs like Costco or Sam's Club).

### Non-Eligible Transactions
The following transactions do *not* earn any cash back rewards:
* Cash advances, ATM withdrawals, and balance transfers.
* Purchasing lottery tickets, casino gaming chips, or cryptocurrency.
* Annual fees, interest charges, late fees, or returned check fees.
